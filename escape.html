🕯 The Study

A desk buried under papers, dust, and regret. The lamp flickers like it's trying to say something. A leather journal lies open to the last page. Next to it, a cold coffee cup with something dried on the inside rim. On the wall, a painting of a forest — the trees are wrong. Too many. Too close. Beneath the desk, half-hidden, a torn photograph of someone who looks exactly like you.

🚪 The Corridor

Seven doors. Only one is unlocked — the one behind you. At the far end, a cracked mirror shows a corridor with eight doors. A coat on the hook has a name tag written in handwriting you don't recognize. The scratches on the floor spell something if you tilt your head. Near the skirting board, a folded note has been slid under Door 4.

🧠 The Clinic Room

White walls gone grey. A metal chair. A case file on the table — stamped CLOSED, but the clasp is undone. On the ceiling, written in something dark, is a single sentence you have to stand directly beneath to read.
